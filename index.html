Featured Projects

Project: WP6 (KNOWLEDGE BASE DI FABBRICA)

The Idea of WP6 is to implement the integration architecture of PLM, MES and ERP applications having central repositary system in a real use case and solve the real problems faced when designing and manufacturing a new shop order in a company. To achieve this, it is necessary to understand the work processes, the different stages and the different departments of a company. we were able to have all the necessary information to understand the working process of a Italian company (company name is discrete) which is in the automotive sector and is dedicated to the design and modification of mathematical models and basically the construction of models and prototypes for cars. This research work will help SME's following a one-of-a-kind production to integrate the systems into the company for better efficiency. This project gave oppurtunity to me to work as a PLM designer, Full Stack Developer and acquired great knowledge on database Management, REST API's and Project Management skills.
